# Wonder Cabinet Brand Guide

**Source**: PDF Brand Guide (WonderCabinet_BrandGuide.pdf)
**Designer**: Art & Sons (artandsons.com)
**Date**: December 20, 2025
**Project**: Wonder Cabinet - From the creators of *To The Best Of Our Knowledge*

---

## Overview

Wonder Cabinet is a project from the creators of *To The Best Of Our Knowledge*. The brand identity features a distinctive cabinet icon with a cosmic spiral/galaxy element, representing a "cabinet of curiosities" concept with an otherworldly, exploratory feel.

---

## Primary Logo

The primary logo consists of:
- **Cabinet Icon**: A vintage-style cabinet silhouette with curved top edge and tapered legs
- **Cosmic Spiral**: A white spiral/galaxy rendered in a stippled, starfield style inside the cabinet
- **Wordmark**: "WONDER CABINET" in a distinctive typeface with spaced letters
- **Tagline**: "From the creators of *To The Best Of Our Knowledge*" (italic serif)

### Logo Variants

| Variant | Background | Cabinet | Text |
|---------|------------|---------|------|
| Primary (Light) | Cream/White | Black with white spiral | Green wordmark, black tagline |
| On Black | Black | Cream outline with green drip, cream spiral | Cream text |
| On Green | Green (#10A544) | Black with white spiral | White text |
| On Cream/White | Cream | Black with white spiral | Green wordmark, black tagline |

### Horizontal Logo
A horizontal variant is available with the cabinet icon to the left of the stacked wordmark.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Color | Hex | RGB | CMYK |
|-------|-----|-----|------|
| **Black** | #000000 | 0, 0, 38* | 40, 30, 20, 100 |
| **Green** | #10A544 | 16, 165, 68 | 82, 7, 100, 0 |
| **Cream** | #FFFAEB | 255, 250, 235 | 0, 1, 7, 0 |

*Note: RGB values for black show slight variation in source document

### Usage Guidelines
- Green is the signature brand color, used prominently in backgrounds and as an accent
- Black provides contrast and is used for the cabinet icon body
- Cream serves as a warm alternative to white for backgrounds and light elements

---

## Typography

### Headline Font
**Futura Bold**
- Used for: Headlines, wordmark
- Character set: Full alphabet (upper and lowercase)
- Style: Bold, geometric sans-serif

### Secondary Font
**Adobe Garamond**
- Used for: Body text, taglines, supporting copy
- Character set: Full alphabet (upper and lowercase)
- Style: Classic serif with elegant proportions

---

## Secondary Graphics

### Drawer / Cabinet Labels
Two styles of decorative label frames:
1. **Outlined version**: Simple rounded rectangle with wavy banner at top
2. **Filled version**: Black rounded rectangle with "WONDER CABINET" text

These can be used for:
- Navigation elements
- Category markers
- Decorative accents

### 3D Cabinet Illustration
An alternate perspective view of the cabinet showing:
- Angled 3/4 view
- Letters of "WONDER CABINET" arranged as if on individual drawers
- Small cosmic spiral element
- Detailed cabinet legs and construction

---

## Logo Spacing and Clear Space

The primary logo should maintain adequate clear space around all sides. Use the cabinet icon height as a reference unit for minimum padding.
